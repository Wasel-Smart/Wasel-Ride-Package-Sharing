# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to keep collaboration respectful, direct, and professional. Productive disagreement is fine. Personal attacks, harassment, intimidation, discrimination, and bad-faith disruption are not.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreement.
- Critique ideas, code, and decisions without attacking people.
- Assume good intent until evidence suggests otherwise.
- Protect user privacy and operational security in all discussions.
- Help keep reviews actionable and evidence-based.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or abusive language
- Doxxing or sharing private data without permission
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention
- Repeated bad-faith arguments or deliberate disruption
- Publishing security issues publicly before responsible disclosure

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to `support@wasel.jo`. Include links, screenshots, and enough context to investigate. Reports will be reviewed confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors who violate this policy.
